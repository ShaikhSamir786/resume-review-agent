# Profile Summary — Interview Questions

**Summary**: B.Tech (IT) student and aspiring Full Stack Developer with hands-on experience in backend development using Node.js, Express.js, and both SQL and NoSQL databases. Skilled in building secure REST APIs, GraphQL APIs, real-time apps, and integrating third-party services. Experienced with message queues, cloud services, and modern DevOps tools. Committed to clean code, systems thinking, and continuous learning.

---

## Backend Tech Lead Lens

### Technical
1. You describe yourself as "aspiring" Full Stack Developer. What's the gap between where you are now and where you need to be to drop that word? What's your plan to close it?
2. You list "systems thinking" as a commitment — give me a specific example of a system-level problem you solved where the fix wasn't just writing code.

### Behavioral / Career
3. Why Full Stack rather than pure Backend or pure Frontend? What draws you to both sides?
4. You're still a student — what convinced you to invest in a 9-month internship at Logicwind alongside your degree? How did you balance both?

## Full Stack Engineer Lens
