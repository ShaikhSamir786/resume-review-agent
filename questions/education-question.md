## Backend Tech Lead Lens

### Technical
1. Your CGPA is 7.99/10. How does your academic performance reflect your engineering ability? Was there a particular semester where your grades improved or dropped — what changed?
2. What courses in your B.Tech IT program were most relevant to the backend work you do in your projects? Which were least relevant?

### Behavioral
3. How did you balance your 9-month internship at Logicwind with your coursework? Did your grades change during the internship period?
4. You're graduating in 2026. What's your plan for the gap between your internship ending (May 2026) and graduation — are you looking for full-time, another internship, or do you have ongoing academic commitments?
