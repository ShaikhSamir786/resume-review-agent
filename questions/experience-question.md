# Logicwind — Backend Developer Intern — Interview Questions

**Duration**: Sep 2025 – May 2026  
**Location**: Surat  
**Stack**: microservices, Firebase, Dittofeed, Reoon Email Verifier, OneSignal

---

## Backend Tech Lead Lens

### Technical
1. You worked across microservices, Firebase, and scalable backends. Walk me through the architecture of ONE product you worked on. What was the service boundary? How did services communicate? What was your specific contribution?
2. You integrated Dittofeed, Reoon Email Verifier, and OneSignal. Walk me through the integration with ONE of them — what API did you use, what data did you send/receive, how did you handle failures, and what was the hardest part?
3. You mention "migrated packages." What was the most technically challenging migration? Was it a library upgrade, a database migration, or a service migration? What broke and how did you fix it?
4. At Logicwind, were services deployed with Docker? How was CI/CD set up? Were you responsible for any part of the deployment pipeline?

### System Design
5. You worked across "microservices, Firebase-based apps, and scalable backend architectures." Describe the deployment topology — were these separate products or part of a platform? How did they share data, if at all?
6. If you had to rebuild ONE of Logicwind's products from scratch, what would you do differently based on what you learned from the existing system? Be specific about architecture, technology, and process changes.

### Behavioral
7. Your resume says you "strengthened problem-solving and systems thinking skills." Give me a specific example of a problem at Logicwind where you had to think beyond code — a trade-off, a design decision, or a production issue.
8. You were an intern. Walk me through a situation where you disagreed with a senior developer's approach. How did you handle it? Were you right?

## Full Stack Engineer Lens

### Technical
