# Education — Section Reviewer

Evaluates the depth, completeness, relevance, and presentation of the Education section.

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Relevance** — does the degree/field align with the target role?
- **Completeness** — are degree, institution, dates, and GPA included?
- **Signal strength** — does GPA help or hurt? Are relevant coursework or achievements highlighted?
- **Formatting** — is it scannable and consistent with the rest of the resume?

## What to Look For
- Missing graduation date or "In Progress" status (candidate must clarify timeline)
- Low GPA that may screen out at companies with GPA filters (typically <3.0/4.0 or <7.0/10)
- Schooling details (high school) that are no longer relevant once university is listed
- Relevant coursework, honors, dean's list, or academic projects that strengthen the signal
- CGPA formatting — is it properly aligned and easy to parse?

## Common Issues
- Including high school details after 2+ years of university (usually unnecessary)
- GPA below cutoff without context or upward trend
- No relevant coursework or academic achievements
- Graduation timeline ambiguous (no month/year)
- No mention of extracurricular tech activities (hackathons, coding clubs, research)
